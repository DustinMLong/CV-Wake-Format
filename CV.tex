\grantinfo{R01MH115754}{Merlin (PI)}{07/01/18--06/30/23}
{Evaluation of the Efficacy and Mechanisms of a Novel Intervention for Chronic Pain Tailored to People Living with HIV}
{The overall objective of this proposal is to evaluate a novel theory-based Pain Self-Management intervention, “Skills TO Manage Pain” (STOMP), developed for and tailored to people living with HIV (PLWH). This study also investigates the impact of a chronic pain intervention on disease-specific HIV outcomes in addition to pain and function.}
